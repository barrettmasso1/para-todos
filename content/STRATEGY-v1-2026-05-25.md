## 1. The Core Strategic Bet

This is a **movement fundraise**, not a major-gift campaign.

The political math demands it:
- **3,500 signatures** from registered voters of the proposed territory (33% of voter roll)
- **2/3 YES vote** in the plebiscite
- **2/3 of BCS Congress** approves
- Plus baseline tests: 5,000+ inhabitants, 3,000+ in cabecera, "consultation" with La Paz cabildo (who will likely oppose)

You cannot pass those three gates without **deep, broad community ownership**. A 5-anchor-donor strategy raises the money but loses the movement. A 2,000-small-donor strategy raises the money AND mobilizes the signatures AND legitimizes the eventual political ask.

**Every peso raised in Stage I is a vote we already have.**

## 2. The Two-Document Strategy

We don't merge the Manus deck and the donor pitch — they're different tools for different conversations:

| Document | Audience | Purpose | When used |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Manus deck v2 (augmented)** | Community / stakeholders / town halls | Exploratory conversation starter — "is this even something we want?" | Weeks 1–8 stakeholder + community engagement |
| **Donor package** | Anyone who's said "yes, I want to support this" | Conversion to financial OR non-financial support | Triggered when someone moves from Listener → Supporter |

The Manus deck **never asks for money**. The donor package **only goes to people the Manus deck has already convinced**. Keeping these separate preserves the exploratory framing and avoids the political trap of "this was always a fundraising scheme dressed as a conversation."

## 4. Donor Package — Stage I Launch (600K MXN target, 300K immediate)

### 4a. Landing page on `paratodos.mx` (or new subdomain)

**URL:** `paratodos.mx/futuro` (suggested)
**Hero:** *"A Conversation About Our Region's Future — Help Start It"*
**Subhero:** *"Stage I: 600,000 pesos. 2 months. Funded by our community."*
**Live progress bar:** updates in real time as donations come in

**Page structure:**
1. **Hero + CTA** — donate / volunteer / share
2. **The 60-Second Version** — what we're exploring, why now, what your support funds
3. **The Plan** — embedded Manus deck OR direct link
4. **The Budget** — Stage I line items with checkboxes (✓ as items get funded):
   - Promoter Committee formation: 200K
   - Public opinion survey (600 questionnaires): 240K
   - Boundary delimitation cartographic study: 160K
5. **Donation tiers** (suggested, not enforced):
   - **50 pesos** — Pueblo supporter
   - **500 pesos** — Vecino activo
   - **5,000 pesos** — Community builder
   - **50,000 pesos** — Stage I patron
   - **500,000 pesos** — Founding underwriter
   - **Custom amount** — any value accepted
6. **Non-financial support form** — checkboxes for: signature gathering, town hall hosting, translation, design, video, social media, accounting, legal review, advisory committee, other (free text)
7. **Transparency promises**:
   - Public quarterly financials
   - Public expense ledger
   - Stage II not raised until Stage I deliverables published
   - PARA TODOS A.C. board independent of any business interest
8. **FAQ** — addressing the predictable concerns:
   - "Will my taxes go up?" → No on federal/state, municipal possibly different
   - "Will I still be able to own/sell property?" → Yes, unchanged
   - "Is this an outsider land-grab?" → Resident-led, governed by PARA TODOS A.C., open books
   - "What if it fails?" → Stage-gated, we stop if community doesn't want to continue
   - "Who decides the boundaries?" → Community input + technical study by Vita Stage I
   - "Who's Vita Insumos?" → Mexican consultancy hired to manage process, doesn't decide outcome
9. **Founding Committee** — names, photos, statements

### 4b. Payment infrastructure

- **Mexican peso donations:** PayPal Mexico + OXXO Pay + bank transfer to PARA TODOS A.C. account
- **USD donations:** Stripe + PayPal international (accept conversion at posted rate)
- **Crypto optional:** if there's expat support for it
- **In-person:** physical donation events with QR-code-based ticketing tied back to the live progress bar
- **Tax-deductibility:** PARA TODOS A.C. is a Mexican civil association — check whether it has CLUNI registration for tax-deductible status (donatariaautorizada). If not, that's a Stage I admin task. US donors may need a fiscal sponsor (e.g., Global Giving partner) for US tax deductibility — explore in week 2.
